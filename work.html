Our Programs

RAHI Pathshala

RAHI Pathshala is a community-based learning initiative for children of ragpickers and migrant families living in urban slums. The program provides foundational education, moral values, and life skills through activity-based learning and personalized attention, helping children build confidence and prepare for mainstream education.

Women Empowerment

Our Women Empowerment program focuses on improving the socio-economic status of rural women through farm and non-farm livelihood opportunities, skill development, financial literacy, and entrepreneurship. The initiative enables women to become self-reliant and active contributors to household and community development.

Natural Farming

RAHI Foundation promotes Natural Farming practices that are eco-friendly, cost-effective, and sustainable. By reducing dependency on chemical inputs and improving soil health, farmers enhance productivity, profitability, and long-term resilience while contributing to environmental conservation.
