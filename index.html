Our Mission

To create a just and equitable society by empowering vulnerable communities through innovative and sustainable development programs in education, livelihoods, gender equality, and resource management.

Our Vision

A world where every individual has access to basic rights, sustainable opportunities, and a dignified life free from discrimination and inequality.

About RAHI Foundation

Established in 2025, RAHI Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to addressing critical social challenges faced by marginalized communities. We focus on long-term empowerment, self-reliance, and community-led development.

What We Do

Education – RAHI Pathshala

Providing foundational learning, life skills, and a nurturing environment for children of migrant and ragpicker families.

Women Empowerment

Supporting rural women through self-help groups, financial literacy, leadership training, and livelihood opportunities.

Natural Farming & Livelihoods

Helping farmers adopt eco-friendly farming methods that improve soil health and increase income.

Natural Resource Management

Promoting afforestation, water conservation, and sustainable agricultural practices.
